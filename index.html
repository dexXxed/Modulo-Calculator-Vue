Modulo operation

In computing, the modulo operation finds the remainder after division of one number by another (sometimes called modulus).

More info

Shortly can be written as:

x mod y = r, (y ≠ 0)

Where:

x - the divident

y - the divisor

r - the modulo (0 ≤ r < |y|)
